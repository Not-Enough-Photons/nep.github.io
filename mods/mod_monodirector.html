MonoDirector

Release: 2023 TBD

Game: BONELAB

Platforms: Valve Index, Meta Quest 2

Overview

MonoDirector is a mod for BONELAB that lets you take recordings of scenes.

You can record yourself as an actor, and play back the movements that you made to see how your performance looks!

The creativity is yours. Make a fight scene, a shootout, car chase, or a drama. Everything is possible with MonoDirector.

WHO ARE YOU... ALL?

Not Enough Photons was founded by me, adamdev, or just Adam. It mostly is a solo effort, where I am the only one that develops the mods, games, and even the stuff you are seeing on this website.

The name came from a random thought I had when I came up with an idea for a game. I wanted to make a developer name up. Something that will be new to me, and memorable. Not Enough Photons came about one night while I was in the kitchen of my house, and I ran with it ever since.

Most of the work is done by my own hands, but I also tend to get other hands to help out to make projects that needs to be done!
